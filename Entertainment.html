Bored Yet?

So what is there to do on a tropical Island? Taniti island has plenty of things to do from going fishing at the docks or taking a tour boat around the island all the way to hiking one of the many trails on the island. You can even get to experience the islands history at the local museum or go join some of the local natives Toko Tuesdays!


Fishing Trip!

Get your gear and head to the docks. You can fish off the docks, from the beach, or if you are feeling up to a challenge. You can go with Grulk, the island fisherman, and go to the Kracken Den. *Must be proficient with cannons.


Going Hiking? (Stay on the path)

After making a stop by the local guild, you can hike some of the many trails on the island. The guild memebers will stick with you to the end.

    • The Legend of Taniti

    According to some ancient texts in the Temple on the island. The natives would worship their gods and the high god was named Lekann. Lekann ruled over the other gods and would appear to the natives as a child. He would bring gifts and laughter where ever he went. According to legened the island is extremly sacred. As it was the last resting place for the gods. The gods were at war with a great darkness and in the final days of the gods they had seeked shelter among the natives and had a temple build deep in the center of the island. However, the way to enter the temple was lost to time. Somewhere hidden on the island are sacred keys that each of the gods created to protet each of their magical artifacts that would give a native god like abilities. As of current the most known item is the Dagger of Toko.
