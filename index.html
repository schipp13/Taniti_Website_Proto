About The Island

Taniti Island is a luscious and magical getaway from your everyday life. You can relax on the beach with your family, go fishing from the docks or out at the Kracken Den with the local island fisher Grulk. You can hike the islands many trails through the rain forest. However, if you go hiking in the forest. Please go to the local guild and take a trail guide with you otherwise good luck! The island also has a historcial museum that will provide a tour and tell you more about the history of the island. If you get hungry, assuming you are human, we have many places for you to eat at. There is Toni's barn, The Seperants Tail, and The Screaming Pumpkin. After a long day fighting your way through the islands ancient dungeons you will need some rest. The island provides a varity of hotels for the islands guest.
